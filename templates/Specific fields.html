OCR Application

OCR technology is used to extract text from images or documents, making data management more efficient for businesses by converting information into machine-readable text.

Importants of OCR

OCR is crucial for businesses as it streamlines workflows by digitizing large volumes of paperwork such as forms, invoices, legal documents, and contracts.

It is particularly valuable in industries like healthcare, legal, finance, and retail for tasks such as patient records, document analysis, transaction processing, and inventory management
